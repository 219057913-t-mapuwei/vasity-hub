# Vasity Hub Rules and Modules

## Core rule
Students self-register. All staff accounts are created by the Super Admin or elevated admin flow.

## Modules
- Authentication
- Student profile
- South African student details
- Foreign student details
- APS and university-specific APS
- Career guidance
- Recommendations
- Course search
- University search
- Applications
- Documents
- Payments
- Premium services
- University review
- Billing
- Financial reports
- Refunds
- Admin/user management

## Foreign student details
Passport number, citizenship, visa status, study permit, medical cover, SAQA/USAf status, translations.

## Colour direction
South African-inspired: green, gold, black, red, blue and white, used in a modern professional way.
